Our Donate Plan

Online donation is a new way of social assistance. We know very well that People has five basic needs. but many of people deprived from getting all those needs. We try to do charity by three way for fulfilling minimum three basic needs.

Food for a family

Food is the most important basic needs for a human being. When people get hungry they need food. without food we can not survive properly. To get food, some people commit crime and try to robbery, hijack even try to kill people. So we do charity by giving food to hungry people or a family or flood victims specially in flood area. Besides we do charity by giving food to street hungry people also who are hungry from long time.

Shelter for a family

Home is another basic important need for all creatures. Every creatures need a home to live. There is a lot of people who are homeless. We try to do charity to homeless people by giving them shelter. Specially to senior citizens who has no relatives in this world. We build shelter home and give it to homeless people. We also provide food to them. In our shelter Home, lot of people are living and surviving.

Education for a child

Education is the fundamental basic needs for all nation. Education is the backbone of a nation. Education for a child is very very important. without education we can not get proper job or work. So from childhood, a child need proper Education. When they get proper education, they will get enough knowledge to get job. There is a lot of child who did not get education for lake of money. So we do charity for child education.
